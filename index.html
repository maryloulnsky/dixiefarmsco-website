Our Menu

We serve up Southern comfort on a plate—with bold Louisiana flavor and seasonal specialties sourced fresh from the Gulf Coast and beyond. Our menu items rotate throughout the year and availability can vary by event.

Seafood Boils & Shellfish

  • Seasonal Crawfish Boils – with corn, potatoes, and sausage (when in season)
  • Alaskan Bairdi Snow Crab Clusters
  • Prestige Oysters – raw or grilled when available

Links, Rolls & Cajun Classics

  • Boudin Links – crawfish or pork
  • Sausages – Andouille or alligator, seasoned
  • Sausage Rolls – on fresh-baked bread
  • Shrimp Salad Rolls – chilled shrimp tossed in creamy Cajun dressing, served on a fresh roll
  • Crab Salad Rolls – sweet crab salad with a hint of lemon and spice, served on a fresh roll
  • Crawfish Salad Rolls – Louisiana crawfish in a tangy house-made mix, served cold on a roll

Bowls & Bisques

  • Shrimp, Sausage & Corn Bisque – rich and creamy with a Cajun kick
  • Étouffées – crawfish and shrimp, smothered in traditional Cajun roux over rice
  • Gumbos – hearty, dark-roux gumbo with seafood or chicken and sausage

Sides & Snacks

  • Zapp’s Chips – straight from Louisiana! Flavors include Voodoo, Regular, Spicy Crawtators, and Cajun Crawtators.

Desserts

  • Mawmaw’s Apple Cake – a rich, spiced apple cake baked from scratch with love.

Catering & Private Events

Whether you're planning a backyard bash, mountain wedding, corporate retreat, or lively festival, Dixie Farms delivers an unforgettable Cajun experience. We specialize in full-service seafood boils with crawfish, shrimp, Bairdi crab, rich sides, and Mawmaw’s Apple Cake—all served from our 40-foot purple trailer or set up family-style. From rustic to upscale, we tailor the menu, setup, and vibe to match your event perfectly.
